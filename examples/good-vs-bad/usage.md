# Good vs Bad Usage

## Good

- "Build a `tihai` in `teental`, `chatusra`, 1 cycle and show equation + segments."
- "Create `cert_mock` for `ABGMVM` `MADHYAMA_PRATHAM`, 15 questions."
- "Plan practice for 4 days/week, 20 min/day, fatigue high, missed 2 days last week."

## Bad

- "Give me any random tihai" (missing taal/jati context).
- "Certification exam for any board" (missing board/level for mock mode).
- "Practice plan" (missing available time constraints).
